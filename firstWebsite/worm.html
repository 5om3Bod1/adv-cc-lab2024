Earth Leviathan

Danger Rating: 4/10

My two cent: Catches you when you least expect them

Details

The Earth Leviathan occasionally spawns and roams around the map underground. When it comes across a player, the worm will begin an attack. The player will experience the found begin to rumble and see dirt flying up. Soon after the worm will fly out of the round eating whatever is above it.

Tips

If you were to be attacks its best to drop all the items you can and run straight forwardws. The worm can't move when its attacking so there is no need to worry about it tracking you as you run forwards.
