Bunker Spider

Danger Rating: 2/10

My two cent: Try to stay away from it

Details

Bunker Spider like other indoor monsters spawn from vents. They tend to set up webs where they spawn, making more and more webs over time. Making it really hard to move around the facility if it's blocking a main route. If you walk into a web the Spider will be trigger and come after you.

Tips

Takes a few melee attacks to kill the spider, but becareful since it two shots players. You can use melee weapons to break webs in one swing.
