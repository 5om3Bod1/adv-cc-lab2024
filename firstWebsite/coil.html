Coilhead

Danger Rating: 9/10

My two cent: Goodluck getting out

Details

Coilhead like most other indoor monsters spawn from vents. They have a very distinguishable running noise, that almost sound like pitter pattering. You must look at the Coilhead or else it's allowed to move towards you. Coilheads move extremely fast so if you: one don't realize its coming you way or two loose line of sight. There's a good chance your dead. Dealing with a Coilhead isn't where the diffuculty comes in. It's having to deal with it while dealing with other monsters at the same time that makes it deadly.

Tips

Have you and your teammates take turns looking at the Coilhead as you all try to escape. Communication is key here, telling eachother which way to backup to and is there any dangers to be mindful of since they are focused on the Coilhead.
