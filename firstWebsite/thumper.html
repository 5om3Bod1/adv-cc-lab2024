Thumper

Danger Rating: 7/10

My two cent: Run around corners

Details

Thumper like other indoor monsters spawn from vents. You can hear them crawl around the facility by their disinct crawling noises. Which sound like a massive body of flesh being dragged around. When spotted by the Thumper, it will proceeds to storm at the player ramping up in move speed. If it crashes into a wall, the Thumper will be stunned for a moment.

Tips

Utalize corners when trying to escape the Thumper. Try to never run in a straight line from the Thumper. Using a melee weapon can be a way to kill it, as successful hits resets its move speed.
