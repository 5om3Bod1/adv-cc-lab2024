Hygrodere

Danger Rating: 1/10

My two cent: "Can you move any slower.."

Details

Slimes like other indoor monsters spawn from vents. They travel extremely slowly towards players. It takes slimes to hits to kill a full health player so don't worry about getting one shot.

Tips

Use railings to get around slimes as they can't hit you when you're on it. It is possible to sprint jump over slimes, so remember that when you're being chased and run into a slime.
