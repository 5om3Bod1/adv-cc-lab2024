Danger Rating: 6/10

Details

Masked can be found on the high tier moons, along side its items. The Masked also known as the mimic, looks like a player expect with a theater mask on. They can be found aimlessly walking the facility aslong as there is player in it. If the game detects there is no player in the facility the mimic can leave the facility and head towards the ship. At times even stay in the ship waiting for players to return. When you are killed by the mimic, your body becomes a mimic now hunting your teammates.

Tips

Using a melee weapon can easily dispatch of the mimic, but becareful of your spacing. Step a little to close, it might spell your end. Also a teammate can help smack the mimic off of you when they latch onto you.
