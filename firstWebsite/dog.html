Eyeless Dog

Danger Rating: 6/10

My two cent: shhhh..

Details

Eyeless dogs in their name can't see, but they have extremely good hearing. Relying only on thier hearing as a means of locating players. Best when you see a dog is to remain quiet and if they are far enough quickly warn teammates of the dog.

Tips

The dogs can be easily baited by a teammate either through proximity chat or any form of sound. Even better is if you have the money to spare, buy a walkie talkie from the store. Wait until the package drops creating a lot of noise to attrach the dogs. Making it easier for you and your teammates to get back into the ship.
