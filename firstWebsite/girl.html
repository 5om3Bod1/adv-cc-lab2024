Ghost Girl

Danger Rating: 9/10

My two cent: Go back to ship and pray

Details

The Ghost Girl can occasionally spawn on the higher diffuculty moons. The girl will proceed to haunt one player. Going through multiple stages of being passive. To its final stage of strolling towards the player, then proceeds to murder them on impact. Once the girl successfully kills its victim, it will move on to another player and repeat the process.

Tips

If you see the girl, its best to look for the exit and stay in the back(in relation to the ship exit) of the ship.
